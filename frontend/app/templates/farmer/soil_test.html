How soil testing works
  1. Book a test and collect a soil sample from your field (instructions provided by the lab).
  2. Mail or drop off your sample at the chosen Extension office.
  3. Results arrive in 1–4 weeks — enter them here to unlock crop suggestions.
  4. CropPulse combines your results with live Rio Grande water data and drought conditions to recommend the best crops for your land.
