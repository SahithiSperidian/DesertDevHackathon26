Reading Your Lab Report

pH: Most NM soils are alkaline (7.5–8.5). Chile, onion, and lettuce prefer 6–7.5.

Nitrogen: "Low" means the soil can't feed a heavy feeder crop without amendments.

Phosphorus: NM soils often have adequate P but poor plant availability due to high pH.

Salinity: Common in lower Rio Grande valley soils. High salinity limits many crops.
